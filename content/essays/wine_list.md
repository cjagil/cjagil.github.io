6/25 - Lewes, DE
    - Monthelie, Clos du Meix Garnier Monopole; Monthelie-Douhairet-Porcheret; 49.95
        - Color: light, smoky red on the edges.  Garnet, purple in the center.
        - Nose: floral? not heavy on the alcohol. Its 13.5 percent, but not reading that way.  Feels light and springy.
        - Taste: Incredible.  Bright, acidic. Playful. Like a just ripened cherry.  Very reminiscent of gamay and beajoulais, but a little grown up.  Still shops at Pac Sun, but not Tilly's anymore.
        - Rating: 8.5/10
